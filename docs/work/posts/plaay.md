## What we did (Zein's notes)

We did a bunch of renders for their products. That's it. Pretty cool, we had fun. They described the product to us and we created it for them in AI.

The wild part is they didn't even have the product ready yet. So we used AI to ideate. They got to see and test their packaging before they ever had it sitting in front of them. That's the actual story here, not "AI photography." It's "AI lets you start before you have the thing."

## Why this is worth a post

Most product photography posts go: "we shot the product." This one goes: "the product didn't exist yet and we shot it anyway." That's a different kind of story. It's the closest thing to time travel a brand gets.

For brands building something new, this changes the order of operations. You can test packaging with a real audience before you commit to print. You can run ad creative before manufacturing. You can find out if people actually want it before you've spent six figures finding out.
