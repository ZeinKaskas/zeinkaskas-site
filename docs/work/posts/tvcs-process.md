### The Workflow

Four steps:

1. **Script and storyboard.** AI-assisted scriptwriting and visual storyboarding.
2. **Asset generation.** Generating characters, environments, and scenes.
3. **Animation and motion.** Bringing static assets to life with advanced motion models.
4. **Editing and post.** Final assembly, color grading, and sound design.

### The Output

> "Broadcast-ready commercials produced in a fraction of the time. Full control over style, tone, and messaging, with rapid iteration capabilities."
