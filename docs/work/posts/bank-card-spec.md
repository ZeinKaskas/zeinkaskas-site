## What we did (Zein's notes)

Another spec ad. This one is crazy. The whole concept is that we really understand how social platforms work now. People's attention requires re-hooking every single second. You don't get the next second of someone's time for free, you have to earn it. That's exactly what this video does.

Every beat re-engages. Every cut earns the next. That's the bet, and that's why it works.

## Why this is worth a post

Most ads are still made like TV ads. A single hook at the start, then a cooldown into product info, then a CTA. That doesn't survive a feed. On TikTok, on Reels, on YouTube Shorts, you don't get sustained attention. You rent it, second by second, and the rent comes due constantly.

This spec is built for that reality. The post is the chance to show people what "earning the next second" actually looks like in a finished piece, and break down where the re-hooks are. Beat by beat.

This is also one of the strongest pieces in the portfolio. Worth pushing.
