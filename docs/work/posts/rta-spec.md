## What we did (Zein's notes)

Spec commercial for the RTA. We were pitching them. We used Seedance and a bunch of different elements to put it together. It was really cool and we had a lot of fun making it.

The angle for this post is to show all the different elements we used to make it work. Not just the final cut, but the layers underneath. That's what makes the post interesting, because most people only ever see the final commercial, never how it was assembled.

## Why this is worth a post

Spec work is honest. Nobody paid us to do it, we just wanted to. So when we made it, we got to make exactly what we'd want to make for RTA if we'd been hired. That's pure taste, no compromises. That's a useful thing to put in front of a future client.

It's also a behind-the-scenes opportunity. The post can break down the elements: characters, environments, motion, edits, sound. The way it actually came together. Most reels show the cake. We're showing the kitchen.
